Poke Bowl

Description

This delicious bowl has a white rice base and is topped with grilled salmon, avocado, mango, cucumber, nori, and edamame.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Grilled Salmon
  • Half of an avocado
  • A few mango chunks
  • Cucumber slices
  • Two nori leaves
  • A handful of edamame
  • (Optional) A sauce of your choice

Steps

  1. Prepare your white rice on a stove or rice cooker.
  2. While we wait for the rice, begin grilling your salmon on a grill or stove.
  3. Place your white rice at the base of a bowl.
  4. Cut your grilled salmon into slices and spread them on the rice evenly.
  5. Cut some mango slices and spread them around your bowl.
  6. Cut your avocado and place the slices off to a side of the bowl.
  7. Cut your cucumber slices and place them in the bowl.
  8. Add your two nori leaves.
  9. Finish it off with a handful of edemame seeds.
  10. (Optional) Add soy sauce or eel sauce for extra flavor!
